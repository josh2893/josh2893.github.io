About the Game

Pixel Runner is a fast-paced HTML5 arcade game where you guide a living pixel courier through the glitching expanse of the Neon Drift. Chain Pixel Orbs to build your combo, grab Bomb Pickups to clear hazards, and snag the rainbow Star Orb to become invincible for a heartbeat of pure speed.

Combo cap: x5 Bombs: press Space Dash: hold Shift Move: WASD / Arrows

Gameplay Features

Run & Dash

Instructions

Collect orbs to score and raise your combo. Bombs wipe out spikes in range. Star Mode lets you plow through hazards — use it to reposition, rescue a combo streak, or grab risky pickups.

Move: WASD / Arrows Dash: Shift Bomb: Space Pause/Restart: P/R

Lore: Runners of the Neon Drift

Pixels are people. In the Neon Drift — a shattered data-galaxy — each pixel is a tiny citizen carrying memory, music, and map-fragments of lost worlds. They gather into Orbs to travel safely between serverscape islands.

You are a Runner, a courier working for the Archivist. Your job is to guide Orbs through hostile lanes, dodging rogue Arrows — corrupted seek-and-delete routines that evolved predatory instincts.

Bombs are rescue flares: compressed rollback packets that unravel nearby corruption with a brilliant detonation of color. Combos represent sync with the Drift — the tighter your rhythm, the more the world rewards you.
